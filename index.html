One Glove is a not for profit mission to reclaim unpaired gloves for those who are in need.

Why?

It's highly likely that if you live in a cold climate area or frequent ski resorts, you have lost a glove. Dropped from the lift while texting, misplaced in the back seat of a rental car, or vanished along with the elusive missing sock -- that glove is gone.

Annoyingly, only one glove goes missing leaving the other lonely. But you keep that single glove in hopes that you'll find the other.

Let's face it -- it's not coming back.

FAQ

Can I mail mittens or liners?

Of course!

I have a complete pair of gloves that I no longer need. Can I mail those?

Of course!

Who does this benefit?

We deliver matched gloves to those in need in the Denver metro area. This includes homeless shelters, Goodwill, Salvation Army, or simply directly to a person who needs gloves during the colder months.

Can this be expanded outside of Denver?

Ideally yes but our resources are limited. Please contact us if you'd like to help out in other areas.

I have no gloves to contribute, can I make a monetary donation?

We appreciate the offer, but we do not accept any money. Please consider making a contribution to your local shelter most of which are tax deductible.

Will you accept socks?

Not yet, but it's on the radar!
